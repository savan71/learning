Our Services

Best in class Service & Product provider

We offer the services for Furniture raw materials like Plywood, Flush Door, Block Board, Laminate, MDF and many more. We know that a furniture project can take too long and is definitely tiresome, time consuming and completley out of the field for many of us. So to ease your work, we take complete interior furniture projects for any of your premises which relieves your pressure by saving your precious time and money.

PLYWOOD

The heart of any Furniture project is Plywood, so it needs to be best in quality with a longer life and strength which can be efficient and effective. ZENSTAR offers you the same and due to that, we are the leading Retailer and Wholeseller of Plywood all over Gujarat since our Establishment. It includes both commercial and Waterproof(PF-710) plywood

FLUSH DOOR

Flush doors are found to be the guards of our premises and so it needs to be the strongest. ZENSTAR Flush doors comes with triple layer, complete thickness and Waterproof(PF-710), the main reason why they are in demand for many of our Distributors and Builders across Gujarat.

BLOCK BOARD

Block Boards are the strongest of all and it needs to be as they are used several times everyday. ZENSTAR provides the best quality A grade Block Boards which are strongest of all other stuffs and that is what makes it different from others.

OTHER STUFFS

There are a number of other things that are required rather than just Plywood, Doors and Block Boards for furniture. We also deal with other required stuffs such as Bidding Patti, Laminates, MDF, Veneer, Cement Sheets, PP Sheets, Korean and many more. No need to go anywhere seperately for the other required stuffs.
